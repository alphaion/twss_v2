Who we are

A monthly meet book club curated by @fandoms.novels and @misswriterreads. Woolf is often admired for her pioneering essays on artistic theory, literary history, women's writing, politics of power, and especially internal experience of alienation. In its intermingling of separate consciousnesses, Virginia Woolf's fiction is both intellectually and psychically challenging.
